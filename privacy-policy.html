Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains how Roja ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, discloses, and protects the personal information of users ("you" or "your") of our personal financial health mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. By using our App, you consent to the practices described in this Policy.

Please take a moment to read this Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal information.

1.1. Information You Provide:

When you use our App, we may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, including but not limited to:

  • Your name
  • Contact information (e.g., email address, phone number)
  • Financial information (e.g., bank account details, bank statements, credit score)
  • Employment and income information
  • Location data
  • Social Security Number or government-issued identification numbers
  • Device information (e.g., device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)

1.2. Information Collected Automatically:

We may also collect certain information automatically when you use our App, such as:

  • Usage and transaction data
  • Device and network information

1.3. Cookies and Similar Technologies:

We may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect information about your usage patterns and preferences. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

2. Use of Your Information

We may use the collected information for various purposes, including but not limited to:

  • Fetching data from your bank statements
  • Processing and managing cash advance applications

  • Complying with legal obligations

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We may disclose your personal information to third parties for the following purposes:

  • Consent: We may share your information with your consent.

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no data transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. We cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us.

5. Your Choices

  • Opt-Out: You may opt out of receiving promotional communications from us.
  • Data Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal information, subject to legal and operational requirements.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
